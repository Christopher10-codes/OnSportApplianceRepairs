Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our appliance repair services.

What areas do you service?

We service all residential areas in: Gqeberha, Jeffrey's bay, Grahamstown, Human's drop, Uitenhage and areas nearby these towns, we are mobile. Contact us to confirm if we cover your area.

How quickly can you respond to a service call?

We guarantee a response within 25-30 minutes for every call, with most repairs scheduled within 24 hours. Emergency services are available 24/7.

Is there a call-out fee?

There is no call-out fee if you approve our quotation we proceed with the repair.

What brands do you repair?

We repair all major brands of home appliances including but not limited to Samsung, LG, Whirlpool, GE, Frigidaire, Bosch, Maytag, and more.

Do you offer any warranty on repairs?

Yes, all our repairs come with a 6-month guarantee on both parts and workmanship. If the same issue recurs within this period, we'll fix it at no additional cost.

Do I need to buy parts separately?

No, we source all necessary parts and include them in your repair quote. Our technicians come prepared with commonly needed parts to complete most repairs in one visit.
